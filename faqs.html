Divine Royal International College admits students from junior secondary through senior secondary levels but we welcome inquiries to discuss individual cases

We are a full boarding school dedicated to providing a supportive, safe, and immersive learning environment for all our students.

Our boarding facilities include comfortable dormitories, study areas, dining halls, recreation areas, health facilities, and 24-hour supervision by house parents, nurses and staff on duty
