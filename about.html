Our Mission

We are committed to producing the total child in character and learning through the provision of qualitative formal education, combined with the right Christian ethics and principles.

Our Vision

To provide an equal, stimulating, and conducive environment where students can:
Identify and achieve their fullest potential.
Become torchbearers in moral and academic disposition.
Become drivers of positive change in their generation.

Our Policy

It is our policy to ensure that students:
Have a safe experience in school, which includes immediate and effective response to bullying or any other anti-social behaviors.
Maximize their academic potential and enjoy their educational experience.
Make a positive contribution, develop relationships, and build confidence.
