News & Events

Welcome to the 2024/2025 Academic Session...

New Student Resumption/Orientation

September 7, 2024

On September 7, we welcomed new students with a comprehensive week-long orientation program designed to help them transition smoothly into our school environment. The program introduced them to our values, academic expectations, and resources, ensuring they felt at home. By the end of the week, these young learners were well-prepared to begin their journey with confidence and enthusiasm.

Returning Students

September 10, 2024

September 10 marked the return of old students, who were happy to reconnect with friends and dive back into school life. Familiar faces filled the campus, bringing a sense of continuity and excitement for the term ahead

Resumption Test

September 11, 2024

Old students took part in a resumption test on September 11, designed to review and refresh their knowledge from the previous term. This assessment helped set a strong foundation for the academic challenges of the new term.

New Parents’ Orientation

October 5, 2024

To introduce families to our school’s mission and approach, we hosted a New Parents' Orientation on October 5. The orientation included an overview of our programs, values, and expectations, setting a strong foundation for family partnerships throughout the year.

Teachers’ Day Celebration

October 5, 2024

On the same day, we celebrated Teachers’ Day, expressing deep gratitude for our dedicated educators. Their passion and commitment were recognized with appreciation from students and parents, making this celebration truly special.

Open Sunday

October 12, 2024

Our Open Day on October 12 welcomed parents and guardians to engage with teachers, observe their children’s work, and celebrate student progress. The event fostered a collaborative spirit, strengthening the partnership between school and families to support student success.

Valedictory Service and Legacy Gifts

October 13, 2024

The Valedictory Service on October 13, 2024, was a memorable event as we celebrated our graduating students who achieved outstanding academic excellence. Special recognition was given to those with exceptional academic achievements and to the student awarded for exemplary behavior. In a touching show of gratitude, our graduates donated mattresses to the school as legacy gifts, leaving a meaningful impact on their alma mater.

Mock Examinations for Grade 12

October 24, 2024

Grade 12 students sat for their first JAMB mock examinations on October 24. This crucial step in their preparation allowed them to gauge their readiness and experience the test environment firsthand. We commend their hard work and dedication as they prepare for the official examinations..

Visit from Sisters Fellowship International

October 25, 2024

On October 25, members of Sisters Fellowship International visited our school, bringing food supplies for the community . This generous gesture emphasized the values of compassion and support that we hold dear at Divine Royal International College...

Half-Term Break

October 26 to November 2 2024

Today marks the beginning of the Half-Term Break, giving students a chance to rest and recharge. We look forward to welcoming everyone back on November 2, ready to continue the academic journey with renewed focus and enthusiasm...
