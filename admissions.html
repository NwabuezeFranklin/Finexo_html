Admission Requirements

Junior Secondary School (Grades 7, 8 & 9):

  • Previous Education: Completion of primary education or equivalent.
  • Placement Assessment: Entrance exam covering core subjects.
  • Documentation: Submission of previous school records and a completed admission form.

Senior Secondary School (Grade 10):

  • Previous Education: Completion of Junior Secondary School or equivalent.
  • Specialization Streams: Options include Science and Arts.
  • Placement Assessment: Entrance exam to determine readiness and placement.
  • Documentation: Submission of academic transcripts, a recommendation letter, and a completed admission form.

Documents Needed: Academic transcripts, birth certificate, recent passport photos, immunization records.

Health Information: Medical clearance or health records as required.

Note: Admission depends on space availability and alignment with our academic and behavioral standards. For further details, please contact our admissions office.
